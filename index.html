About NHY

Building Futures Through Talent, Learning and Community Action

New Horizon for Youth (NHY) is a youth-led community-based organization founded in Kakuma Refugee Camp.

We believe that every young person deserves opportunities to learn, grow, innovate, and contribute positively to society regardless of their background or circumstance.

Through integrated programs in education, skills development, entrepreneurship, arts, sports, culture, and environmental stewardship, NHY is creating pathways for sustainable community transformation.

Vision

A Kakuma where every young person thrives in a clean, healthy environment while discovering their talents and building a meaningful future.

Mission

To empower youth through integrated programs that transform talent, knowledge, and purpose into lasting community impact.
